Your inbox is now automated

Here's a plain-English summary of what's live, what's coming, and the two things we need from you to finish.

How it works

1

Email arrives at support@ or info@

The system picks it up the moment it lands — no manual checking needed.

2

AI reads and categorises it

Every message is classified in seconds: is it a product question, a health concern, an order issue, or a business inquiry? What language is it in? Does a human need to respond?

3

Your team gets notified in Slack

The right channel gets a summary: who sent it, what it's about, and a direct link to reply in Gmail. No inbox digging.

4

Every weekday at 8am — morning briefing

A summary of unread messages is posted to Slack so your team starts the day knowing exactly what's waiting.

What we need from you

1 — FAQ list

To enable automatic replies, we need your approved answers to the most common customer questions. A simple Google Doc or email is fine. Questions we'd expect to cover:

  • What are the ingredients in each product?
  • Is it vegan / gluten-free / allergen-free?
  • How much to take and when?
  • How long until I see results?
  • Which countries do you ship to?
  • How long does delivery take?
  • What is your return policy?
  • How do I track my order?

Important: the system will only use answers from this list — it will never improvise or guess. If a question isn't covered, it routes to your team instead.

2 — Booking / consultation link

You mentioned wanting to move away from Calendly. Once you've decided on the new tool (or confirmed you're keeping Calendly), send us the link and we'll update it straight away.

Safety & data

What we will never do

  • Send any unsolicited content to your customers
  • Generate or suggest medical advice of any kind
  • Auto-reply to health questions — these always go to a human
  • Store raw email content — messages are processed and discarded
  • Send anything outside the approved FAQ list once auto-reply is enabled
