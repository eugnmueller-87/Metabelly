## HOW TO USE FIBER

- **Dose:** 1 level tablespoon (10g) in 200ml water or cold/lukewarm non-carbonated drink
- **Timing:** Morning before meals or between meals — same time daily for consistency
- **Maximum:** 10g morning + 10g evening (do not exceed)
- **Wait time:** Add powder, wait 10 seconds for it to dissolve slightly, then stir
- **Can I add it to:** Water ✓, smoothie ✓, juice ✓, yogurt ✓, cold/lukewarm non-carbonated drinks ✓
- **Avoid:** Hot drinks, carbonated drinks
- **If bloating occurs:** Start gradually, drink enough water, monitor — if persists consult a doctor

## SCIENCE & BENEFITS

### What resistant dextrins do
- Resist digestion in small intestine → reach large intestine → feed beneficial bacteria
- Increase Bacteroides concentration (8-10g/day for 14 days)
- Reduce Clostridium perfringens (potentially harmful bacteria) at 15g/day
- Fermentation lowers colon pH → creates favorable conditions for good bacteria, inhibits pathogens
- Produces short-chain fatty acids (SCFA) — energy source for gut cells

### Clinical evidence
- 80% of users report reduced hunger and prolonged satiety
- 66.1% of participants lost weight (avg 2.3kg) in 12-week double-blind study vs 16.4% placebo
- Significant BMI reduction from week 4 onward
- Reduced body fat % at weeks 4, 8, 12 vs baseline
- Reduced caloric intake naturally through satiety effect

### Conditions where fiber has shown benefits (scientific literature)
